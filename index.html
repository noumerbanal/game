SUMS The Arithmetic Game

How to play

  • Fill up the boxes with the right addends that will justify the sums around it.
  • You can only input numbers incrementally starting from 1.

Tips

  • Always check if every addends are equal to its corresponding sum
  • Enjoy and focus while playing the game.
